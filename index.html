About Our Bags

Shopping bags are more than just practical carriers; they are an essential part of modern consumer culture.

Plastic shopping bags are lightweight and inexpensive, but many countries now discourage them because of pollution.

Paper bags are biodegradable and offer a more environmentally friendly option.

Reusable fabric bags are becoming popular because they are sustainable and stylish.

Shopping bags are also a powerful branding tool used by companies to advertise their products.

Bag Terminology

Handbag
A small bag carried by hand or shoulder.
Backpack
A bag worn on the back with two straps.
Travel Bag
A large bag used during trips.
